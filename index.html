What Are Olympiads ?

According to certain websites , Olympiads are exams that are conducted throughout the country that lets students compete with other students on a similar education level.The aim of a good Olympiad exam is to provide a unique competitive platform and identify the young geniuses and create a talent pool for the future.Olympiads are not just only exams; they do a lot more to bring out the best in a child. Trying and giving Olympiads motivates students to strive for better.
